Install esptool using pip or pip3 depending on your system
pip3 install esptool
Erase Current flash (if any) [Change the ports according to your system]
esptool --port /dev/ttyUSB0 erase_flash
Download the correct .bin file for your board from the official MicroPython site. Then run:
esptool --port /dev/ttyUSB0 --baud 460800 write_flash --flash_mode dio --flash_size detect 0 ESP8266_GENERIC-20251209-v1.27.0.bin
